Master the Art of Outfit Prompts

Learn how to create effective prompts for stunning cultural outfit transformations

Describe Outfit Details

Be specific about colors, patterns, fabrics, and cultural elements. For example, "royal blue silk saree with gold zari border" or "cream sherwani with maroon embroidery."

Preserve Identity

Always include phrases like "preserving the face and identity of the person in the input image" to maintain resemblance and personal features.

Specify Quality

Include terms like "photorealistic," "high resolution," "professional photography," and "studio lighting" for better visual quality.

Add Cultural Context

Mention the specific region or occasion to enhance authenticity. For example, "dressed for a South Indian wedding" or "celebrating Diwali in a traditional Gujarati style."

Include Accessories

Don't forget to mention traditional jewelry, headwear, or other accessories that complete the cultural look, like "with a kundan necklace" or "wearing a traditional pagdi."

Sample Prompt Template

Copy and customize this proven template:

"A full-body photograph of the same person in the input image, same face, same expression, same pose, wearing a [dress], with the same background, photorealistic, DSLR quality"
